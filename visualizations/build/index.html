Extrapolating Terrorism, Building a Future Modus Operandi

This dashboard contains three components. Data visualization of the GTD data set, real time terrorism tweets scraper and a dynamic model that looks at the dynamics of the recruitment of terrorists. Furthermore it is possible to check whether the benefits of a new security regulation exceeds the costs. Watch the video for an explanation of how to navigate the website.
